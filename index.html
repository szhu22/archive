HIPPIE MODERNISM: THE STRUGGLE FOR UTOPIA



Archival Club
with Mindy Seu & Jon Gacnik
Berkeley Art Museum
Saturday-Sunday, April 1-2
Deisgner: Siyao Zhu


Overview
What is a digital archive? What is a microsite? How can we subvert the terms? Archival club explored the processes of digitization and collection. The designers created a printed reader for the Berkeley Art Museum's Reading Room and leave with a website showcasing curated content.

Concept
For newer exhibitions, David Wilson collaborates with the curators and mines the exhibition catalogs for referential books that relate to the era of the exhibition.

The microsite is a collection of 24 covers of Hippie Modernism referential books. The books are arranged by color and thickness.
